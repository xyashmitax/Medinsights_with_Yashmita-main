MedInsights Knowledge Check: Skincare Ingredients Edition

Yashmita Sharma June 07, 2025

Think you know your AHA’s from your BHA’s? Test your skincare science knowledge with this quick MedInsights quiz. Choose one answer for each question, submit your responses, and see where your skincare ingredient knowledge stands.

The skincare industry is full of marketing tactics and "miracle" creams that claim to reverse aging but behind every bottle is an ingredient list that tells you what's really happening. Understanding what the basic, primary ingredients in a product do helps you choose products tailored to your needs, avoid falling for marketing tactics, spot red flags such as comedogenic ingredients, and build an effective and science-based routine. You might recognize niacinamide, retinol, and vitamin C, but do you really know what they're doing to your skin? It's time to draw focus on the biology and chemistry behind skincare because great skin doesn't come from pretty packaging but from informed decisions.
